## ✨ Features

### 🤖 AI-Powered Storytelling
- **Google Gemini 2.0** generates unique stories (80-120 words per turn)
- **30 turns** of dynamic narrative
- **Plot twists** at turns 10 & 20
- Stories adapt to your choices and morality

### ⚔️ 10 Anime-Inspired Story Paths
- **Cursed King** (Jujutsu Kaisen) - Domain Expansion, Malevolent Shrine
- **Demon Blood** (Demon Slayer) - Blood Arts, Regeneration
- **Special Grade** (JJK) - Cursed Techniques, Six Eyes
- **Rogue Shinobi** (Naruto) - Rasengan, Tailed Beast Mode
- **Vampire King** - Blood Magic, Immortality
- **One-Eyed Ghoul** (Tokyo Ghoul) - Kagune, Kakuja
- **Chainsaw Devil** - Chainsaw Transformation
- **Hollow King** (Bleach) - Cero, Resurrection
- **Nen Master** (Hunter x Hunter) - Hatsu Abilities
- **Black Swordsman** (Berserk) - Dragonslayer

### 🎯 RPG Mechanics
- **4 Character Classes**: Berserker, Cursed Caster, Shadow Assassin, Blood Priest
- **Advanced Combat**: Type advantages, weaknesses, equipment bonuses
- **14 Equipment Items**: Weapons, armor, accessories
- **10 Achievements**: Track your legendary journey
- **Morality System**: Choices matter (-18 evil, 0 neutral, +12 good)
- **10 Power Levels**: Weak Human → ABSOLUTE POWER

### 🎨 Professional UI
- Netflix-quality dark theme
- Smooth animations & transitions
- Real-time notifications
- Minimizable side panels
- Typing animation for immersion
